Why EduVision AI?

EduVision AI was created to help designers, educators, and developers understand how visual content appears to people with different types of color vision deficiencies. Accessibility is not just a feature — it's a responsibility. By simulating how images look through the lens of protanopia, deuteranopia, and tritanopia, we aim to raise awareness and encourage more inclusive design practices.

Whether you're building a website, designing educational materials, or simply curious about visual accessibility, EduVision AI gives you the tools to analyze contrast, dominant colors, and accessibility ratings across multiple vision types.
